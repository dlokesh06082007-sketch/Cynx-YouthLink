# 🌌 YouthLink: Digital Mental Health Ecosystem
**CodeNyx Hackathon | Track: HealthTech & Social Impact**  
*Building a world where asking for help is strength, not fear.*

---

## 📌 Overview
**YouthLink** is a comprehensive support system explicitly designed for adolescent mental health and emotional wellbeing. It bridges the critical gap between youth facing extreme academic/social pressures and established outreach organizations (like the Youngistaan Foundation). 

By providing anonymous help-seeking channels and early intervention mechanisms, YouthLink ensures that the 90% of youth in low-income settings who currently receive absolutely no psychiatric care finally have a safe, dynamic entry point to targeted support.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🧑‍🎓 For Youth (User Dashboard)
- **Stigma-Free Environment:** A carefully engineered, trusted digital space for youth to express emotional concerns natively without fear of judgment.
- **Nature Prediction Queries:** Youth-friendly digital wellbeing tools that contextually assess the user's state to provide personalized resources in real-time.
- **Safety Switch:** A discrete emergency quick-exit feature to ensure physical privacy in environments where mental health support is not yet normalized or accepted.
- **Peer Mentorship Networks:** Highly accessible 1-1 volunteer-led support structures deeply rooted directly within the community.

### 🛡️ For Mentors & NGOs (Sentinel Portal Dashboard)
- **Professional Oversight:** A high-fidelity, data-driven dashboard for NGOs to actively monitor large-scale youth wellbeing indicators and provide swift early intervention.
- **Early Warning Triage:** Predictive algorithmic mechanisms to actively detect and respond to semantic early warning signs before issues silently escalate.
- **1-1 Networking Hub:** Secure, encrypted, anonymous uplink channels allowing mentors to manage help-seeking requests and provide direct, empathetic text support.
- **Impact Analytics:** High-level structured data insights (like Stability Matrices) used to inform geographical outreach and accurately measure the organization's macro social impact.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js 16, HTML5, and Tailwind CSS for a blazing fast, responsive, "Anti-Gravity" professional user interface.
- **Backend:** Node.js & FastAPI for heavy secure data processing and hosting the AI-driven "Nature Prediction" analysis.
- **Real-Time Uplink:** WebSockets to dynamically power anonymous 1-1 networking and instant communication.
- **Database Architecture:** PostgreSQL for strictly managing structured, encrypted telemetry data on youth wellbeing indicators.
- **Security Protocol:** End-to-end encryption to fiercely maintain and guarantee the anonymous "Safe Space" promise for all active youth participants.

## 🏁 Automated Redirection Logic
Upon a successful login, the system algorithm validates the user's operational role:
- **Youth Login (User):** Bypasses standard routing and redirects instantly to the youth-friendly wellbeing toolset and safe space environment.
- **Mentor Login (NGO):** Redirects seamlessly to the NGO Wellbeing Dashboard (Sentinel Portal) granting professional administrative oversight and data monitoring privileges.
